Who We Are

Started in a Small Studio, Now in 12,000 Homes

HomeNest was founded in 2019 by Ishita and Rohan, two architecture graduates with a shared passion for beautiful, purposeful spaces. What started as a weekend market stall in Hyderabad has grown into a loved home décor brand trusted across India.

We travel to artisan villages, work closely with potters, weavers, woodworkers, and metalcraftsmen to bring you pieces that carry real stories — objects made by real hands, with genuine skill and care.

Every item in our collection is personally selected for quality, design, and authenticity. We believe your home should reflect who you are, and we're here to help you make it so.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make — from which products we stock to how we treat our artisan partners

Artisan First

We work directly with craftspeople, paying fair prices and supporting their livelihoods. When you buy from us, you support a real artisan family.

Sustainable Sourcing

Natural materials, eco-friendly packaging, and responsible supply chains. We strive to make beautiful décor without harming the planet.

Uncompromising Quality

Every piece is inspected before it reaches you. We only sell what we would proudly place in our own homes.

Community and Trust

We build long-term relationships — with our artisans, our customers, and our communities. Trust is our most valued asset.

Thoughtful Curation

We carefully select pieces that are truly special, meaningful, and designed to last.

Love in Every Detail

From how we photograph products to how we pack your order, we care deeply about every detail of your HomeNest experience.
